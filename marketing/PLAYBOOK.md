## What this does NOT include (deliberately)

These are the lines I didn't cross. None of them would be ethical/legal/safe for MNKB.

| Tactic | Why not |
|---|---|
| Fake Google reviews | FTC 16 CFR §465 makes this illegal as of 2024. Google permanent map removal when caught. |
| Fake GBP listings (multi-listing or virtual office abuse) | Permanent map removal; verifiable when Google traces ownership signals. |
| Schema stuffing (fake AggregateRating without real reviews) | Manual penalty + violates your own memory rule. Audit script confirms current schema is clean. |
| PBN networks | High infrastructure cost, easily detected, manual penalty when one node burns the rest. |
| Negative SEO | Illegal (computer fraud), easily traced via referring domains, traumatic if you're caught. |
| Auto-generated bulk parasite content | Site Reputation Abuse policy kills this. The 3 parasite articles in this playbook are first-party expert content, which is the surviving format. |
