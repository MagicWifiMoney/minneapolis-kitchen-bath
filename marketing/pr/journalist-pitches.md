## Sending Notes

- Use Muck Rack or the outlet masthead to confirm the right reporter and current beat before sending. Beats shift quarterly.
- Personalize the first sentence with a reference to a recent piece the reporter wrote. Generic openers get deleted.
- Send Tuesday through Thursday, 9 to 11 AM Central. Avoid Monday morning and Friday afternoon.
- One follow-up only, then move on. Multiple follow-ups burn the relationship.
- Track every send, response, and outcome in the table at the top of this document.
- Never pitch a client story without written consent from the homeowner. Keep signed releases on file.
